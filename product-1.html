Decaf-Costarica
TDH Coffee® is the name we give to our rarest small-lot coffees. And this holiday season we’re blending two of them to create an unforgettable cup. Building on the inspiration of last year’s inaugural TDH Coffee® Christmas offering, this year’s blend joins two extremely limited coffees: Hacienda Alsacia™, grown at our very own farm in Costa Rica, and hand-selected aged Sumatra. Both are exceptional in their own right, and both are roasted artfully at our TDH Coffee® Roastery and Tasting Room in Seattle. Our master blenders have forged a vibrant, modern expression of the holiday—with savory spice notes and sweet brightness, it is the gift of extraordinary flavor, a celebration of all the season’s beauty.
